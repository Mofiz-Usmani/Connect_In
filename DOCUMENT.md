**ConnectIn** is a platform for students to **learn, share, and collaborate**.  

### Core Features:
- **Domain-Specific Discussion Boards**: Structured forums for students from different fields.
- **Study Buddy Matching**: Pair students with complementary skills or similar goals.
- **Student-Centric Social Media Layer**: A feed for academic/professional updates.

### Unique & Differentiating Features:
- **Gamified Learning & Collaboration**
  - Badges for contributions.
  - Leaderboards for top contributors in different domains.
- **AI-Powered Knowledge Assistant**
  - Summarize long notes.
  - Suggest resources.
  - Auto-tag uploaded notes for easy search.
- **Cross-Domain Hackathons & Challenges**
  - Collaborate on real-world problems.
  - Example: CS + medical student build a health-tracking app.
- **Interactive Notes & Smart Libraries**
  - Collaborative annotation.
  - Multimedia support (videos, diagrams, voice notes).
- **Skill Portfolio & Micro-Credentials**
  - Showcase projects, certifications, contributions.
  - Issue badges for collaborative tasks.
- **Integrated Career & Internship Hub**
  - Companies/professors post opportunities.
  - Students apply with their platform portfolio.
